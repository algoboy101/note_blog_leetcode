<pre>+---------+------------------+------------------+
| Id(INT) | RecordDate(DATE) | Temperature(INT) |

|       3 |       2015-01-03 |               20 |
|       4 |       2015-01-04 |               30 |
+---------+------------------+------------------+</pre>

<p>例如，根据上述给定的 <code>Weather</code> 表格，返回如下 Id:</p>

<pre>+----+

|  2 |
|  4 |
